adapter-payments – my notes
----------------------------

what I built and why:
the problem was OrderService was directly calling FastPayClient and SafeCashClient with
different method signatures, so adding a new payment provider meant touching OrderService
every time - bad. I introduced a PaymentGateway interface with one method: charge().
then wrote FastPayAdapter and SafeCashAdapter - each one just wraps its SDK and translates
the charge() call into whatever that SDK actually needs. now OrderService only talks to
PaymentGateway, doesnt care which provider is behind it. added a new provider? just make
a new adapter and drop it in the map. OrderService never changes.

patterns used:
- Adapter (main pattern - bridging incompatible interfaces)
- Constructor injection (pass the map of gateways into OrderService)

challenges:
SafeCash was the tricky one - it doesnt have a single pay() call, it does createPayment()
first and then you call confirm() on the result. took me a minute to figure out I needed
to chain those two inside the adapter. also used Objects.requireNonNull in both adapters
and OrderService to guard against null inputs like the hints said.
