adapter-payments assignment notes
----------------------------------

what I did:
made FastPayAdapter and SafeCashAdapter to wrap the two SDK clients behind a common
PaymentGateway interface. wired both into a map in App so OrderService doesnt need
to know which provider its talking to at all.

patterns used:
- Adapter pattern
- constructor injection

challenges:
honestly the SafeCash one tripped me up a bit - the SDK splits the charge into
createPayment() and then confirm() separately so I had to chain them inside the adapter.
took me a second to realize thats just how that SDK works lol. also kept second-guessing
whether to put the map in App or OrderService but constructor injection in OrderService
felt cleaner so went with that.
